Top three activities to do at Duddhi

Take a long drive
to Satghumanwa

See the sunrise at
the Thema Banks

Visit the highs of
Lauwa Pahadi

The Satghumanwa is a long spiral of road with 7 rounds to complete (hence the name) with lots of greenery with great views while on a drive.

The Thema Bank depicts a great view of natural beauty along with a temple and place to watch the sunrise with chirps of birds.

The Lauwa Pahadi provides a scenic view of the world that we live in, following the highs, you could see the bigger picture of life.
